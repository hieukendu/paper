\subsection{Result Summary for the Research Question}

% TRACE: S5-C10 | answer/results/main_pragmatic.json; answer/results/p0_visobert_baseline.json; answer/results/p0_multi_seed_ablation.json | bounded RQ summary only
The recorded evaluation supports two bounded conclusions. First, the strongest recorded main-comparison result belongs to Vistral-7B SFT, not to the proposed multi-task encoder; ViSoBERT is now an evaluated three-seed baseline. Second, the P0 ablation records multi-seed configuration differences on the fixed pragmatic test set, while the external diagnostics remain distinct, qualified evaluations. Together, these findings position ViPragSent as a traceable empirical Vietnamese pragmatic-sentiment evaluation rather than a model-superiority result. Source-stratified and low-resource analyses are considered separately in Section~\ref{sec:analysis}.
